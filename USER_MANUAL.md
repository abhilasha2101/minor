## 1. Getting Started

### Creating an Account
To access personalized features like saved articles and your verification history, you need an account.
1. Click the **User Icon** on the top navigation bar.
2. If you don't have an account, click **Sign In / Register**.
3. Fill in your preferred username, email, and password. 
4. Once registered, you will be automatically logged in.

### The Navigation Bar
The top navigation bar is your main hub:
- **Feed**: The primary news discovery page.
- **Verifier**: The core AI fact-checking engine.
- **Current Affairs**: Interactive quizzes and flashcards for media literacy.
- **Community**: See what claims other users are asking to be verified.
- **Theme Toggle (Sun/Moon icon)**: Switch between Light and Dark mode.

## 2. Navigating the News Feed

The **Feed** is designed for rapid, swipeable consumption (similar to short-form content).
- **Scroll** down to view the latest curated news articles. A "Scroll to Top" arrow will appear in the bottom right corner to quickly take you back to the top.
- **Swipe Gestures (Mobile)**: Swipe left or right to dismiss articles or save them for later.
- **Read More**: Tap on an article to expand it and read the full text.
- **Bookmark**: Click the Ribbon/Bookmark icon on any article to save it to your Profile.

## 3. Using the Fact Verifier

The **Verifier** is the heart of Veritas AI, allowing you to instantly check the authenticity of any claim or news image.

### Verifying Text Claims
1. Navigate to the **Verifier** tab.
2. In the text box, type or paste the claim you want to check (e.g., "Did NASA find superfluid helium on the Moon?").
3. Click **Initiate Deep Scan**.
4. The AI will process the claim and return a Verdict:
   - 🟢 **VERIFIED TRUE**: Supported by reliable sources.
   - 🔴 **LIKELY FALSE**: Contradicted by evidence.
   - 🟠 **MISLEADING / CONTEXT**: True elements, but presented falsely.
   - 🔵 **UNVERIFIED**: Insufficient data available.

### Verifying Images
1. In the Verifier tab, click **Upload Image / Screenshot**.
2. Select an image file (e.g., a screenshot of a suspicious tweet or news graphic).
3. The AI will automatically extract the text from the image and run it through the fact-checking engine.

### Sharing and History
- **Share Results**: Click the **Share** button on the verdict card to natively send the report to WhatsApp, Twitter, or messages (available on supported mobile devices).
- **Recent Checks**: The right sidebar displays your recent verification queries so you can easily review them again.

## 4. Community & Current Affairs

### Community Board
- Navigate to the **Community** tab to see what claims are currently trending or highly requested by other users.
- You can submit your own claims to the community board for public verification.

### Current Affairs (Media Literacy)
- Test your critical thinking skills! The **Current Affairs** tab offers:
  - **Flashcards**: Quick tips on how to spot deepfakes, AI-generated text, and clickbait.
  - **Quizzes**: Interactive scenarios to test your ability to distinguish real news from fake news.

## 5. Managing Your Profile

Your **Profile** is your personal dashboard for managing data.
- **Overview**: View your "Trust Reputation" score, which goes up as you verify more claims.
- **Saved Articles**: Access all the news articles you've bookmarked from the Feed.
- **Verification Log**: View your complete history of AI verifications. 
  - *Privacy Feature*: You can wipe your history at any time by clicking the **Clear Log** button.

### Customizing Your Avatar
1. Go to your **Profile** overview.
2. Click on your profile picture/avatar.
3. Choose from a set of dynamically generated avatars or upload a custom image from your device.

---

## 6. Settings & Customization

### News Interests
1. Navigate to the **Profile** tab and click on **News Settings**.
2. Select the categories you care about (e.g., Science, Sports, Technology).
3. The AI will prioritize showing you articles matching these interests in your Feed.

### Dark Mode
Veritas AI natively supports both Light and Dark themes. Click the toggle in the top-right corner of the screen to switch themes instantly. The app will remember your preference for your next visit.
